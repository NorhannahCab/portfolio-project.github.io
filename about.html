Antoni Gaudí

born in 1852, was a Spanish architect known for his unique and distinctive style. His most famous work, the Sagrada Familia, is a masterpiece of modernist architecture. Gaudí's use of organic shapes and intricate details set him apart from his contemporaries, making him one of the most influential architects of the 20th century.


“The straight line belongs to men, the curved one to God.” -Antoni Gaudí
